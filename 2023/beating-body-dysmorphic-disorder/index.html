Podcast on BDD

I am currently researching Body Dysmorphic Disorder (BDD) and came across this podcast series. It introduces the topic but - perhaps more importantly - has interviews with people who have struggled with BDD and have found relief and better lives.

Worth a listen if this disorder affects you or someone you love!

My name is Gerald Tūruapō Jordan and I have a long interest in and experience with counselling.
