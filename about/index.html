About

If you are looking for my business websites, you can find Therapy Aroha at our Aotearoa New Zealand site or our International site. This site is where I discuss topics that cross over between therapy, spirituality and other things and the reflections are often more personal in nature.

Background

My undergraduate degree was in Theology and then I became an atheist. Since becoming an atheist in 1992, I have explored many things - further explorations of Philosophy, years of meditative practice, readings in Buddhism, and much more. While I do not believe there is anyone in the sky watching us - unless perhaps aliens, as the universe is vast - I do know that there are many things beyond our comprehension (most things are beyond our current comprehension). So, I dabble in anything that might create a positive self, world and interpersonal interactions.

This site is a reflection on my journey.
