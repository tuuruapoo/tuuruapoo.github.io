George

22.01.2025

It doesn’t seem that long -
It feels like yesterday.

Those days of walking through the snow,
the rain,
the heat.

Walking down that alleyway,
the trees overhead.

Coming in the back door,
wondering if you were home.

Seeing you sitting in that old armchair,
leaning forward as you listened to your records of scriptures,
your eyes half closed,
as you focused on the words.
No longer able to see,
but more than able to listen.
